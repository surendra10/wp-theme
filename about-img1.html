Our Customer's Reviews

Barsajeeta Naik


I am glad we have this amazing restaurant in Heidelberg area which serves the best Indian food in a finer dining experience. The drinks are fantastic, starters are excellent especially the drums of heaven. The main course dish Punjabi kukkad is unbeatable, the Dum biryani is divine, the garlic naan is the best in the area. You will not be dissapointed. The manager Suman is absolute tops, takes very good care of the guests and we will be going there everytime we crave Indian food. 100% recommend to anyone looking for an authentic experience.

Shruthi Sridhar


One of the best restaurant in Heidelberg.
It has authentic Indian cuisine. Food is very delicious and tasted so good. Ambience is very nice as well. Staff were very friendly and polite. Highly recommend this place.

Shriyash Dandagawahl


What an amazing place to enjoy authentic Indian food.
It was for the first time, I have tasted authentic Indian food in Heidelberg.
One must definitely visit this place and try the food.
The food was so tasty that we ended up ordering more and more and managed to finish every single dish.
One must try chicken Lababdar, punjab kukkad masala and biryani. Naan was awesome(thickness can be less!). Drinks were good. The portion size was also good.
Overall a great experience.

Stefanie.R


Wir waren am 02.10. hier essen. Wir waren von den Speisen sehr begeistert. Es war alles sehr lecker und individuell gewürzt. Wir hatten zwei vegetarische und zwei mit Fleisch.
